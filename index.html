Our Story

We met at Aliyu Musdafa College Yola, in the Junior Section, around 2015 — JSS 1C.

I didn’t know then that a simple classroom would be the beginning of a love I would carry for years.

Before we even graduated from junior secondary school, I started feeling something deeper. By the time we got to SS1, Safiya had completely stolen my heart.

We’ve shared so many memories in high school — her presence alone could make me lose myself in thought.

But the moment I cherish most happened during Sallah festival at Wetland. It was also my friend's birthday that day — and destiny brought us together.

We had a wonderful time, took pictures to remember that day forever… and made a promise:
"Only death can part us."

Today, our relationship may be in a platonic stage… But our hearts are already writing the future. We’re planning to tie the knot, and I pray the world gets to witness our bond.

From classmates to soulmates.
